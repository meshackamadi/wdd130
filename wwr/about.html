History

Born out of a passion for adrenaline and the great outdoors, our white water rafting service launched in the early 1990s with one mission: to turn wild river rapids into unforgettable adventures. What began as a few thrill-seekers chasing waves has grown into a top-tier outfitter known for epic rides, expert guides, and next-level safety. From first-timers to seasoned paddlers, we take explorers through heart-racing rapids and stunning canyon views—making every trip downriver a story worth telling.

Every journey with us is more than just a ride—it's a full-on experience. With cutting-edge gear, certified guides, and a deep respect for the environment, we blend excitement with exploration. Whether you're chasing your first splash or coming back for your tenth run, we’re here to deliver high-energy adventures that push limits and create lifelong memories.
